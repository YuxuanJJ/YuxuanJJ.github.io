About

I am currently a PhD student in the Visual Information Laboratory at University of Bristol, supervised by Prof. David Bull and Dr. Fan Zhang. My main research interests include deep learning, computer vision and image/video processing.
I obtained MSc (Distiction) in Communications and Signal Processing from Imperial College London, where I did research on Flexible Array Signal Processing, supervised by Prof. Athanassios Manikas. I completed BEng. degree in Information Engineering at Southeast University under the supervision of Prof. Qiang Cheng.
My current research is mainly focused on image/video super-resolution and video compression.
